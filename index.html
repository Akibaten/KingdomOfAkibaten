I created this site as a personal project. I think social media has a great capacity to connect people and do good, but it's beecome pretty exhausting these days >_<. As a person who really values doing creative stuff, this site allows me to post and share my works, create a creative work in the process, and free me from unconsciously changing who I am and my art. I still do have social media though, I just don't use it very often. Here is my instagram:P

Do you know the person you want to be? I think most people can give some sort of answer as a collection of statements like "I want to be a doctor" or "I want to be a mom" or "I want to be kind". I think these are all valid things to want (especially the last given it describes you as a person rather than circumstances like your job), but when I ask people this I am really asking them "can you *feel* the person you are called to be?". In my brain I feel like I meet the idealized version of myself through these snapshots, memories from that life that haven't actually happened, that randomly flash through my brain when I think about certain things.
